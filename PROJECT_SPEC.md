# Overview

GestureSpeak AI is a real-time Artificial Intelligence web application that recognizes Indian Sign Language (ISL) hand gestures using Computer Vision and Deep Learning.

The application captures video from a webcam, detects hand landmarks, predicts the performed gesture, converts it into text, and speaks the recognized text aloud.

The objective is to reduce communication barriers between sign language users and people unfamiliar with ISL.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of people rely on sign language for communication.

Most people cannot understand sign language.

GestureSpeak AI provides an AI-powered bridge by translating hand gestures into readable and audible language in real time.

---

# Objectives

• Detect hand landmarks in real time.

• Recognize Indian Sign Language gestures.

• Convert gestures into text.

• Convert text into speech.

• Display confidence scores.

• Maintain gesture history.

• Provide an educational Learn ISL section.
